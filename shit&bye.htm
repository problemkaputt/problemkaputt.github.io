Shit & Goodbye
1999 by Martin Korth
Shit & Goodbye is a small assembler program that exits Windows.

Put a shortcut to Shit & Goodbye on your desktop to avoid to get through the usual shutdown procedure - no more click 'start', click 'shutdown', select shutdown mode, click 'yes' - just click Shit & Goodbye and you are free!
